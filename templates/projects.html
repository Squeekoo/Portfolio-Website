Capstone Project 1
MuMe

MuMe is a Music & Mental Health Community designed as a safe space for people to publicly express themselves through music to complement written words. Members can use MuMe as a virtual journal as well as a social platform to discover others who may be able to relate to their content. This database-driven website was developed using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Font Awesome, Python, Flask, Flask-WTForms, SQL, SQLAlchemy, Bcrypt, Jinja, JavaScript, JQuery, and Axios, and utilized the Spotify API.
