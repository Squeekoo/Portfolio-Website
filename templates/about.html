About

Why is any of this important? Well, because there may be a quiz at the endblock.

Eldon delaCruz, Jr. was born a Californian and raised as an Oregonian.
He grew up in the beautiful smallish city of Tigard and went off to Oregon State University to study music.
After graduating in 2018, he did absolutely nothing with his degree and worked as a self-contractor DJ for weddings and corporate events.
Two years into his stable career, COVID-19 hit. And as the summer slowly went by, so did his sushi-money.
One morning, as he sat waiting for Rocket League to load, he realized he needed to do something other than
eat, sleep, and do whatever he wanted to do while he waited for the pandemic to be over so he could return to working events.
It was at this moment that he decided to leave the Rocket League main menu and research financially stable careers that
- if another pandemic were to occur -
he wouldn't have to worry about job/financial security.
With his impeccable Google-searching skills, he was drawn to the idea of learning how to become a Software Engineer.
He remembered one of his friends mentioning to him that there were coding bootcamps.
Determined, he discovered Career Karma, and within a week he had called multiple bootcamps, figuring out which one would suit him the best.
Alas, he chose Springboard, and after completing and passing their Prep Course, he enrolled in their Software Engineering Career Track.
Throughout this course, he has learned and applied the fundamentals of
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Node, Express, Python, Flask, SQL/SQLAlchemy, & Testing.
Aside from the technical skills, this course gave him the opportunity to learn a remarkable amount about himself, as well.
This section is getting a bit lengthy, so we won't go into detail about how cool of a guy he is now.
However, if you are at all wondering how cool he is, please feel free to head over to the Contact page to find how to reach him.
You can also reach him through his socials by checking the "Socials" dropdown menu.
